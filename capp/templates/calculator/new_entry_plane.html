Methodology

Flight emissions are calculated using the trip distance, the emission factor (which depends on flight type, fuel type, and altitude effects), and a multiplier based on seat class. Since premium seats take more space, they represent a higher share of emissions. Ground transport to and from the airports is also added using a fixed value of 44 gCO₂/km.

Formula:

E = d × FE × M + GT

where:
d = distance (km)
FE = emission factor (gCO₂e/pkm)
M = seat-class multiplier
GT = ground transfer emissions (44 g/km × distance to/from airport)
